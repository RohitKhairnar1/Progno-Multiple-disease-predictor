Input Data Explanation :

Age: Age of the patient in years.

Sex: Gender of the patient. (0 = female, 1 = male)

Chest Pain Type: Type of chest pain experienced by the patient. (1 = typical angina, 2 = atypical angina, 3 = non-anginal pain, 4 = asymptomatic)

Blood Pressure: Resting blood pressure of the patient in mm Hg.

Cholesterol: Serum cholesterol level in mg/dl.

Fasting Blood Sugar Over 120 mg/dl: Fasting blood sugar level > 120 mg/dl (1 = true, 0 = false)

Electrocardiographic Results: Resting electrocardiographic results. (0 = normal, 1 = having ST-T wave abnormality, 2 = showing probable or definite left ventricular hypertrophy)

Maximum Heart Rate Achieved: Maximum heart rate achieved during exercise.

Exercise Induced Angina: Exercise induced angina. (1 = yes, 0 = no)

ST Depression: ST depression induced by exercise relative to rest.

Slope Of ST Segment: Slope of the peak exercise ST segment. (1 = upsloping, 2 = flat, 3 = downsloping)

Number Of Major Vessels Fluro: Number of major vessels colored by fluoroscopy (0-3).

Thallium Stress Test Result: Results of thallium stress test. (3 = normal, 6 = fixed defect, 7 = reversible defect)

Target: Presence of heart disease. (1 = presence, 0 = absence)
